Description:

This is sales invoice worksheet. There are four items in the invoice: printer ink, paper pack, file folders, and USB sticks. If quantity over 10, the sale price/unit will have a discount of 3%. From above the table, the paper pack and file folders have a discount of 3%. And the Ontario GST/HST is 13%.

Screenshot

Named ranges:

"Total" from F3 to F6; "Subtotal" from F7 to F8.

Formulas:

1. Sale Price/Unit for Paper Pack and File Folders =List Price/Unit-List Price/Unit*Discount 2. Total =Sale Price/Unit*Quantity 3. Subtotal =SUM(Total) 4. Amount Due =SUM(Subtotal)
