Named ranges:

"regular" from H3 to H11; "overtime" from I3 to I11.

Formulas:

1. Overtime @ time & half =Hours worked - 40 2. Overtime @ double time =Hours worked - 40 - Overtime @ time & half 3. Regular Pay =Hourly Rate * Hours Worked 4. Overtime Pay =Overtime @ time & half * (1+50%) * Hourly Rate OR Overtime @ time & half * (1+50%) * Hourly Rate + Overtime @ double & time * (1+100%) * Hourly Rate 5. Total Pay =SUM(regular+overtime)
